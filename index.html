Major winter storm in the east

Officials are doing their best to keep motorists off blizzard-affected roads in Missouri, where visibility at times has been reduced to a quarter-mile and a major roadway has been closed because of snow. The National Weather Service office for Kansas City, Missouri, warned residents to stay off the roads for all but the most urgent needs. Biden signs Social Security law before leaving office

With the stroke of a pen, two Social Security provisions nationwide have been eliminated, as President Joe Biden signed H.R. 82 into law on Sunday, January 5. H.R. 82 is commonly known as the Social Security Fairness Act of 2023, sponsored by former Louisiana Congressman Garret Graves (R-LA-06) and former Virginia Congresswoman Abigail Spanberger (D-VA-07). The Act removes the Windfall Elimination Provision (WEP) for retirees and the Government Pension Offset (GPO) for beneficiaries. The WEP and GPO reduce or eliminate earned benefits for retirees and beneficiaries, which add up to approximately 2.8 million Americans, according to Graves.
